Rice field
The fresh atmosphere with a wide expanse of rice fields is the main attraction of Jatiluwih. The rice fields in Jatiluwih are included in the UNESCO World Heritage list and are an example of the Subak irrigation system, and a manifestation of the Tri Hita Karana philosophy.
Besides having a row of beautiful beaches that have gone global, Bali apparently also has dozens of waterfalls that are quite interesting to visit. Blemantung Waterfall is one of them. The waterfall, which is about 50 meters high, is located in a village called Pujungan, in Pupuan District, Tabanan. The distance is approximately about 48 km from the city center of Tabanan or about 68 km if taken from Denpasar City.

Vihara Dharma Giri

Vihara Dharma Giri

Vihara Dharma Giri
Vihara Dharma Giri is a Buddhist temple famous for its large sleeping Buddha statue, located in Pupuan-Tabanan. The monastery has three different floors that visitors can explore, but since it is a sacred place, there are some rules that we must follow. One is that we have to cover our waists with a scabbard, if you don't bring it, don't worry you can borrow the scabbard from the management side.
